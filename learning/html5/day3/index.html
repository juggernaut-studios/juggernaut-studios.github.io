      - Its a key for decoding the characters to unicode.
      - For example : COPYRIGHT SIGN -- c2 a9 [UTF8] -- U+00A9[Unicode]

      <meta http-equiv="Content-Type" content="text/html; charset=utf-8" />

      <meta charset="utf-8" />
